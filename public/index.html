Privacy Policy

PrivaSee Cam is designed with privacy as its core principle. We collect, store, or transmit no personal data, usage data, or analytics about our users. The app processes photos entirely on your device — no data leaves your phone unless you explicitly choose to share a photo yourself.

What PrivaSee Cam does not collect:

  • No location, GPS, or geolocation data
  • No device identifiers or device information
  • No usage or telemetry data
  • No photos or metadata transmitted to any server
  • No account or profile creation required

Photo metadata stripping: By default, PrivaSee Cam removes all EXIF metadata from photos including GPS location, device make and model, timestamps, and camera settings. You may optionally enable embedding of date-only metadata, which you control entirely. All other metadata remains stripped by default.

Accelerometer data: The device accelerometer is used in real-time solely to orient photos correctly during capture. Accelerometer data is processed in memory and is never stored, transmitted, or retained after the photo is saved.

Subscriptions and payments: PrivaSee Cam uses Apple App Store subscriptions for paid access. All payment processing is handled directly by Apple. PrivaSee Cam does not receive your payment card details, billing address, or Apple ID information. Subscription status is managed through Apple and is not made available to the app developer. For more information about how Apple handles your data, please see Apple's Privacy Policy.
